### 2.7 O Algoritmo de Cálculo — 88° Solar Arc

Esta é a parte **mais técnica e mais copiável** de Human Design. O bodygraph requer **DOIS momentos de cálculo**, não um:

1. **Personality (Conscious, black):** momento exato do nascimento
2. **Design (Unconscious, red):** aproximadamente 88 dias antes (~88° de arco solar, **não 88 dias corridos**)

> "The Design side of the chart uses planetary positions from the moment the Sun was 88 degrees of solar arc earlier than the birth position, which works out to roughly 88 days or about three months before birth." [fonte: roxyapi.com, 2026-05-23]

**Por que é difícil de calcular:**

> "The 88° solar arc is not the same as '88 days before your birthday.' This library does it correctly." [fonte: dev.to, 2026-03-09]

> "Design date calculation is iterative, not arithmetic. You cannot subtract 88 days from the birth date and call it the Design date. The calculation requires the bisection loop." [fonte: humandesignhub.app, 2026-03-10]

**Pseudocódigo de cálculo (fonte: humandesignhub.app):**

```python
def find_design_date(birth_jd: float) -> float:
    birth_sun_lon = planetary_lib.longitude_at(birth_jd, body="sun")
    target_lon = (birth_sun_lon - 88.0) % 360.0
    lo = birth_jd - 95.0
    hi = birth_jd - 80.0
    for _ in range(50):
        mid = (lo + hi) / 2.0
        mid_lon = planetary_lib.longitude_at(mid, body="sun")
        diff = (mid_lon - target_lon + 180.0) % 360.0 - 180.0
        if abs(diff) < 1e-6:
            return mid
        if diff < 0:
            lo = mid
        else:
            hi = mid
    return (lo + hi) / 2.0
```

**Armadilhas comuns (segundo múltiplas fontes):**
- "Subtrair 88 dias" (errado — varia com a excentricidade da órbita)
- "Usar a hora do nascimento" (errado — deve encontrar o momento **exato**)
- Não tratar a fronteira 0°/360° (planeta a 2° vs target 358° = diferença de 4°, não 356°)
- Não validar contra Swiss Ephemeris

> "We've seen apps that simply subtract 88 days from the birth date. During some parts of the year, this can be off by 2-3 days, which means completely wrong gate positions for slow-moving planets like Pluto or Neptune." [fonte: astrology-api.io, 2025-01-12]
